How should chords be displayed?

The app already handles two common chord display styles. Which do you prefer for the PDF?

A — Chords above lyrics (aligned)

Each chord sits directly above the syllable where it changes. Standard chord chart format. Takes more vertical space but is easiest to follow while playing.

Amazing Grace
Verse 1
[G]Amazing [C]grace how [G]sweet the sound
That [G]saved a [D]wretch like me
[G]I once was [C]lost but [G]now am found
Was [G]blind but [C]now I [G]see

B — Chords inline in [brackets]

Chords appear within the lyric line in square brackets at their position. More compact — fits more content per column. Slightly harder to sight-read while playing.
