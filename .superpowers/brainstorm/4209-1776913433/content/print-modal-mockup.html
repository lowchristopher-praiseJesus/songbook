Notes on this design:

  • Columns: 2 / 3 / 4 toggle buttons (default: 2)
  • Font size: slider 7–16pt (default: 10pt)
  • 5 colour pickers — Song Title, Lyrics, Chords, Section Labels, Annotations
  • Clicking a colour swatch opens the native browser <input type="color"> picker (no extra library)
  • Page size: A4 or Letter (landscape orientation is fixed)
  • Settings are not persisted between sessions (same as Presentation PDF modal)
